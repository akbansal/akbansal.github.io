About Me

Hello!! My name is Amit, Thanks for visiting my website. This is my first website and I started this website as a small project to learn and to express my creativity on the web designing. I'm passionate about clean and elegant UI. Design is not just my hobby, it's my passion. I believe that in such an ever-evolving industry it's essential to stay on top of new trends and to build such a design using the latest techniques while maintaining a balance of accessibility and cross browser compatibility with all users.

I live & work out of San Jose, CA but have called several places home including Bangalore & New Delhi INDIA.

More info

Senior Front End Engineer with around 12 years of experience, focusing primarily on HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and on jQuery and with all ecommerce domain including Bank of America On Line Banking and ebay Shopping Cart. I not only have passion to create stunning UI but also have grasp of improving site speed and web performance engineering to provide the first class rendering experience to our customers. Currently expanding my knowledge to contribute in ebay open source web components which are created as fluid and multi device components. Looking for a great work environment and a challenging and innovative project to utilize my skills.
